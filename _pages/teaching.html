Introduction

I am involved in teaching Applied Mathematics, Mathematics Education and Statistics courses at Jordan University College. My teaching focuses on developing students’ conceptual understanding, analytical thinking, and practical problem-solving skills.

I integrate theory with real-world applications to help students better understand how mathematics and statistics are used in data analysis, modeling, and decision-making.
