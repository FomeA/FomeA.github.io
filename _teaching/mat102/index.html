MAT 102 – Ordinary Differential Equations

Mathematical Modelling, Change, Growth & Dynamic Systems

Course Description

This course introduces ordinary differential equations arising from physical, biological and engineering systems. Students learn analytical methods for solving first and second order equations, Laplace transforms, and qualitative analysis of solutions.

Learning Outcomes

Concepts

Understand order, linearity and classification of ODEs.

Methods

Solve first and second order differential equations.

Transforms

Apply Laplace transforms to differential equations.

Interpretation

Use solutions in applied real-world contexts.
